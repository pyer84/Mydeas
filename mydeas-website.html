What We Deliver

Our Services

Comprehensive design and engineering capabilities spanning marine and product sectors — from feasibility through to production.

01
Ship & Vessel Design

Full naval architecture services from feasibility and concept through contract and basic design to production drawings. All vessel types — commercial, workboats, offshore, and specialist craft.

02
Structural Analysis

FEA-based structural integrity assessments, fatigue analysis, and vibration studies for marine structures and mechanical assemblies — compliant with classification society requirements.

03
CFD & Hydrodynamics

Computational fluid dynamics for hull form optimisation, resistance and propulsion analysis, seakeeping, and manoeuvring studies. Reducing fuel consumption and improving performance.

04
Industrial & Product Design

From early concept sketching through CAD modelling, prototyping, and design for manufacture — products that are intuitive to use, engineered to perform, and cost-effective to produce.

05
Retrofit & Conversion

Ballast water treatment, scrubber installation, alternative fuel conversions, and equipment retrofit design. Extending vessel life whilst meeting evolving IMO regulations and ESG targets.

06
Decarbonisation & Green Tech

EEXI compliance, CII strategy, LNG and hydrogen propulsion concepts, shore power integration, and solar-hybrid system design. Supporting the maritime sector's transition to net zero.

How We Work

Our Process

A proven methodology that takes projects from initial brief through to delivery — transparent, collaborative, and on schedule.

01
Discovery

We understand your requirements, constraints, regulations, and commercial context before any design work begins.

02
Concept

Multiple design directions developed and evaluated — feasibility, risk, and cost at the core of every decision.

03
Development

Chosen concept refined through detailed engineering, analysis, and classification society engagement.

04
Approval

Full production drawing packages, class submissions, and documentation — ready for build or manufacture.

05
Delivery

Build phase support, site supervision, commissioning assistance, and lessons-learned documentation.
